Tiramisu Savings Club

A DappCamp Team Project

An Ethereum Blockchain-based Savings Club

About

The Tiramisu Savings Club allows anyone to self-organize a savings club and make community lending possible for friends and family. This is similar to loan clubs like Susu, Tanda, and many others that exist all over the world.


This project was a part of the three-week DappCamp program (Cohort 2 - January 2022). We developed this Tiramisu Dapp with guidance from Preethi Kassiredy, Rajesh Muppalla, and Zishan Sami. We appreciate their technical support and encouragement.

How It Works

1. Start a savings club with up to 3 of your friends. The member who starts a club is the owner of the club.

2. Deposit money to your club! You can deposit as much money as you like and any time.

3. When your turn comes, you can withdraw money from your club savings! If the savings club is to be disbanded, the owner can dissolve it any time. The pooled savings are divided according to the deposit history, and are returned back to each member upon club dissolution.
