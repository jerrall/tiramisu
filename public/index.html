Don't you have a bank account?

No worry! Here is your savings box.

A Shared Savings Box on The Ethereum Blockchain

What you can do

1. You can open a savings box with up to 3 of your friends. Choose one of you as the owner of your box.

2. Start deposit your money to your savings box! You can deposit as much money as you like and any time. Withdrawal is allowed for only one member at each point of time. The turn of withdrawal comes in order.

3. When your turn comes, you can withdraw money from your savings box! When you want to close the savings box, the owner can close it any time. The pooled savings are divided according to the deposit history, and are returned back to each member immediately.
